Things to do in Aurangabad

Witness Bibi-Ka-Maqbara

Often referred to as the "Taj of the Deccan," Bibi-Ka-Maqbara is a beautiful tomb located in Aurangabad.It was commissioned by Aurangzeb in memory of his first wife and bears a striking resemblance to the Taj Mahal.

Visit Chatrapati Shivaji Museum

The Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya,formerly known as the Prince of Wales Museum,houses an extensive collection of artifacts,including ancient sculptures.

Explore Ajanta Caves

Ajanta Caves are a series of 30 rock-cut Buddhist monuments that date back to the 2nd century BCE. Known for their exquisite paintings and sculptures, the caves depict various aspects of the Buddha's life and teachings.

Visit Ellora Caves

The Ellora Caves are a vast complex of rock-cut temples,showcases remarkable carvings, particularly the stunning Kailasa temple, which was carved out of a single.

Visit Daulatabad Fort

Daulatabad fort,Known for its robust defense mechanisms, the fort was considered unconquerable and boasts a series of strategic layouts, from narrow pathways to dark passage

Siddharth Garden and Zoo

Siddharth Garden and Zoo is a sprawling park and animal sanctuary, offering a relaxing escape with beautifully landscaped gardens, fountains, and various animal exhibits.
