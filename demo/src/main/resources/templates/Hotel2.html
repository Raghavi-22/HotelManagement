About us

Welcome to Hotel Vivekanand, your ideal home in the heart of Mumbai, the "City of Dreams." Our hotel offers a blend of modern amenities and warm hospitality, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay. Situated close to Mumbai's most iconic attractions—like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and bustling Colaba Causeway—Hotel Vivekanand is the perfect base for exploring the city. From the serene Elephanta Caves to lively Juhu Beach, Mumbai offers endless experiences for every traveler. After a day of sightseeing, unwind in our stylish rooms or enjoy a delicious meal at our on-site restaurant. Our attentive staff is here to make every moment special, providing personalized service to meet your every need. Experience Mumbai's vibrant culture and charm right from your doorstep at Hotel Vivekanand, where every stay becomes a cherished memory.
