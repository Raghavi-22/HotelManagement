Things to do in Mumbai

Visit Gateway of India

Visit this iconic landmark in Mumbai, an arch monument that has stood since 1924 and overlooks the Arabian Sea. It’s a popular spot for both locals and tourists alike.

Day Out at Marine Drive

Stroll along the famous Marine Drive, also known as the “Queen’s Necklace,” especially beautiful at night with a stunning view of the city’s coastline.

Picnic at Juhu Beach

Enjoy a relaxing day at Juhu Beach, one of Mumbai’s most popular beaches, perfect for picnics, sunsets, and street food.

Elephanta Caves

Explore the ancient Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage site, featuring impressive rock-cut sculptures and historic carvings.

Drive on Bandra-Worli Sea Link

Take a drive on the Bandra-Worli Sea Link for breathtaking views and the architectural marvel that connects two major parts of Mumbai.

Walk Through Markets in Mumbai

Experience the vibrant markets of Mumbai, like Crawford Market and Colaba Causeway, perfect for shopping and exploring local culture.
