About Us

Hotel Vivekanand is your gateway to the historic city of Aurangabad and the awe-inspiring attractions of Maharashtra. Elegantly designed to blend comfort and local charm, Hotel Vivekanand caters to tourists from across the globe. Whether you're seeking cultural exploration or a peaceful retreat, our hotel provides the perfect starting point. Located near UNESCO World Heritage Sites like the *Ajanta and Ellora Caves*, our hotel offers access to some of the most stunning rock-cut temples and ancient art in India. Aurangabad itself boasts architectural gems like the *Bibi Ka Maqbara* and historical marvels such as the *Daulatabad Fort*.

With its scenic surroundings and proximity to natural beauty, including nearby gardens and caves, Hotel Vivekanand promises a memorable experience for families and travelers who appreciate history, nature, and luxury all in one place.
